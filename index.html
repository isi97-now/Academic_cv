Academic profile

I am a PhD student and employee at the LCI (Learner Computer Interaction) Lab Department of Computer Science (IDI), Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), in Trondheim, Norway. With an academic background in communication design, I specialised in interaction design and systems usability. My current research explores the intersection of child–computer interaction, sustainable design for learning, educational frameworks for classroom orchestration, and learning technologies and analytics.
